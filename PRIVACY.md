# Privacy Policy for TheContentor

**Last Updated:** [Date, e.g., June 8, 2026]

This Privacy Policy describes how **TheContentor** collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our application to post videos to TikTok. 

This application is a personal/limited-access integration designed solely to facilitate the automated or manual uploading of videos from your local device/server directly to your personal TikTok account via the official TikTok API.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Because this application functions strictly as a tool to post content to your own TikTok account, we limit data collection to the absolute minimum required to perform this function:

- **TikTok Authentication Data:** When you log in via TikTok OAuth, we receive access tokens and refresh tokens. These tokens allow the application to securely communicate with the TikTok API on your behalf.
- **Video Content and Metadata:** To post videos to TikTok, our application processes the video files, titles, descriptions, and tags that you explicitly select or provide for upload.
- **Public Profile Information:** Depending on TikTok's baseline authentication requirements, we may have access to basic public profile details (such as your TikTok username or profile picture), which are used solely to confirm within the application interface that you are logged into the correct account.

**We do not collect, store, or track any personal identification information (PII) beyond what is strictly necessary to authenticate and execute the TikTok API requests.**

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to fulfill the core functionality of the application:
- To authenticate your account via the TikTok API.
- To upload and publish your selected video content to your personal TikTok account.
- To maintain and refresh API connection sessions so you do not have to log in every time you upload a video.

**We do not sell, rent, or lease your data to third parties. We do not use your videos or account data for advertising, analytics, or profiling purposes.**

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Tokens:** Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored securely (e.g., in a secure environment variable, encrypted local configuration file, or secure database) and are never exposed publicly.
- **Video Files:** Video files are processed transiently for the sole purpose of uploading them to TikTok. Once the upload is successful or fails permanently, any temporary copies of the video files processed by the server/application are deleted.
- **Security Measures:** We implement industry-standard administrative and technical security measures to protect your access tokens and content from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

## 4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

Our application interacts directly with **TikTok**. Your information (video data and metadata) is transmitted securely to TikTok's servers to complete the publishing process. 

Your data is governed by **TikTok's Terms of Service** and **TikTok's Privacy Policy**. We highly recommend reviewing their legal terms:
- [TikTok Terms of Service](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/row/terms-of-service/en)
- [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/row/privacy-policy/en)

We do not share your information with any other third-party services, analytics networks, or marketing platforms.

## 5. User Control and Data Deletion

You retain full control over your data and app connections at all times:
- **Revoking Access:** You can revoke this application's access to your TikTok account at any time directly through your TikTok mobile application or web settings under **Settings and Privacy > Security and Login > Manage app access** (or the equivalent setting provided by TikTok).
- **Data Erasure:** If you wish to delete any locally cached authentication records, you can simply log out of the application or delete the local storage/configuration file containing your API tokens.

## 6. Compliance with Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly target, collect, or solicit data from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable legal age in your jurisdiction).

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our application or API requirements. Any updates will be posted directly to this file or repository with an updated "Last Updated" date.
