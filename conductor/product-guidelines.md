# Product Guidelines

## Brand Messaging
*   **Tone:** Professional, innovative, and efficient.
*   **Focus:** Emphasize automation, intelligence (AI-powered), and cost-effectiveness (on-premise, open-source).
*   **Keywords:** Automated, AI-driven, Content Creation, Video Generation, Cost-efficient, On-premise, Scalable.

## Visual Identity
*   **Design Goal:** Clean, modern, and intuitive user interfaces (implied by Blazor usage).
*   **Consistency:** Maintain a consistent visual style across all components, especially within the Blazor UI.

## Content Strategy (for generated content)
*   **Engagement:** Content should be optimized for audience engagement, as determined by AI analysis.
*   **Quality:** High-quality video and audio output with synchronized subtitles.
*   **Customization:** Provide options for voiceovers, subtitle styles, and background videos where applicable.
